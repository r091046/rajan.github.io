About Me

I have over 10 years of diverse experience in the automotive domain, specializing in a wide range of areas, including embedded software development, system testing, cybersecurity, and penetration testing for connected vehicles.

My expertise spans several roles such as:

  • Embedded Software Development - Designing and implementing robust embedded systems for automotive applications.
  • Unit Testing - Writing and conducting tests to ensure software reliability and quality.
  • Requirement Testing - Analyzing and validating system requirements to ensure they meet customer and regulatory standards.
  • System Testing - Conducting comprehensive testing to ensure the functionality and performance of entire systems.
  • Test Analysis - Reviewing and analyzing test results to identify improvements and potential issues.

Over the years, I have expanded my focus to the cybersecurity aspect of the automotive industry, particularly in the area of connected vehicle systems. I specialize in:

  • Cybersecurity Risk Analysis - Identifying potential security risks and implementing strategies to mitigate them.
  • Penetration Testing (PEN Testing) - Assessing the security of vehicle communication networks and identifying vulnerabilities.

Throughout my career, I’ve been committed to delivering secure, reliable, and high-performance solutions that help drive the future of connected and autonomous vehicles. With a strong focus on innovation, I continue to strive for excellence in all of my work.
